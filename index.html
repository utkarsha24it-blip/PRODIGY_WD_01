Our Story

Three Generations
of Baking

La Miètte began in a small Paris suburb in 1987. Grandmother Colette's recipe book — scribbled in pencil, stained with butter — is still the heart of everything we bake today.

We believe in slow fermentation, seasonal fruit, and the kind of patience that can't be rushed. Every morning at 4 am, the oven wakes before the city does.
